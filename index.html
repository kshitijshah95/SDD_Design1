Services

This is what we do. These are performed by our best.

Site Feasibility

Our Feasibililty Study aim to objectively uncover the strengths and weaknesses of the site to record the sizes and conditions that may directly or indirectly affect the service position(Electric panels/towers, Telecom, Air Conditioning outdoor units, Water Supply, Drainage etc).

Site Survey

We provide CAD drawing package that shows the site's length, height and width along with all existing approaches or openings, stuctural features, surrounding man-made & natural elemanets. This survey is accurate for it's figures and drawing package is accompanied by a compiled document of site photogaphs that acts as a visual reminder and assistance while Planning and Design.

Site Layout Planning

On every project, our Site planning mission is to deliver a solution that both functions successfully for users and cpntributes architectural value to it's environment. SDD begins the site layout planning with sketches over the site survey, works to scale using block diagramsand drawing techniques with all the anthropomeric and ergonomic standards well placed and considers vastu too on orienting rooms for the desired use, natural day lighting and adjacent space functions.

Building Design

Each of our buildings stand for their minute details to add prominent value to its size, structure, surrounding, sustainability and cost. The facades of the building are bold and beautifully crafted with seamless yet sturdy material and construction methodologies. We are constantly evolving and updating ourselves with the latest technologies paving the way for marvels in architecture and design.

Landscape Architecture

Working with the natural surrounding that an area has to offer, environmental enhancements are created to produce dynamic designs with pleasant combinations of flora and fauna, rockery, light, wood, wicker, water and innovatives by our expert associates.

Interior Space Planning

We specialize in spatial planning and flow through; capatalizing on all aspects of functionality and practicality prior to developing the creative side of dedicated areas.

Interior Design

A passion for creating special places shapes our design approach. Our team addresses design of each space with the objective of improving productivity through innovative visuals impact and ease of use. The strategy of material selection and application is completely based on a pre thought concept and forms in plan.

Property Branding

Utilizing structural features, Branding elements and themes, we create environment that onsider the human need for information, identity and way finding. By making information meaningful and accessible in space , we can foster a sense of well-being, confidence and even delight.

Graphic Design

Whether the literature your company uses in online or print media, be it a QSR or brochure design that explains your company and services, our Graphic Designers can make all the difference in what people think when they first see or pickup something that has been developed just right for your unique identity.

Project Management

The PMC partners of SDD schedule timescale and cost with definitive targets. The resource allocation, execution management and quality control are all co-ordinated well which reflect in the work progress reports time to time during execution.

MEP Design

Our associate consultant engineers know how to resolve your cares of mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing, HVAC and Fire Protection needs. They can device and avail you with latest energy saving and environment friendly technologies, apt execution and perfectly functional sevices at your premise.
